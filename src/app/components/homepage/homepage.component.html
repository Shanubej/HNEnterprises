Our Origin Story

When sun control films were banned in India, millions of car owners were left searching for a safe and legal alternative to beat the heat and maintain privacy. It was then that Mr. Jai Prakash Mandal, the visionary founder of H.N Enterprises, stepped forward with a bold solution.

He invented the concept of car sunshades in India, offering a practical and stylish replacement that met both comfort and legal standards.

Since its founding in 2012, HN Brand has grown into a trusted name in the industry — known for its custom-fit magnetic sunshades, roller blinds, and premium printed sunshades. Today, we proudly serve as OEM vendors to leading automotive brands, with a strong presence across dealerships and retail markets nationwide.

“There’s not a place for ‘no’ in my dictionary.”
— Mr. Jai Prakash Mandal, Founder, H.N Enterprises

His words continue to inspire our team as we innovate, improve, and lead the way in sunshade solutions made for Indian roads and conditions.
